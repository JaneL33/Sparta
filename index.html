Jane's Color Palette

What do these colors remind you of?

Sky Blue
Best for summer outfits!

Sky Blue is often associated with a sense of calmness and tranquility. It reminds people of clear skies and sunny days. Many summer outfits feature this refreshing color.

Forest Green
Perfect for nature lovers!

Forest Green is a color that symbolizes nature and the outdoors. It is commonly associated with forests, trees, and the beauty of the natural world.

Sunset Orange
Captures the warmth of sunsets!

Sunset Orange is a vibrant and warm color that brings to mind the beauty of sunsets. It conveys a sense of energy and enthusiasm, often associated with positive emotions.
